About Me

My name is Kyle and I am a TAS teacher studying at ACU Strathfield, majoring in Textiles and minoring in Food Technology.

These are my main teaching areas:

I have had the opportunity to teach at a variety of schools in my local area during my placements, including St Leo's Catholic College, Mercy College Chatswood, and Glenwood High School. During these experiences I have had the opportunity to teach a variety of classes in years 7-11, in subjects across TAS including Textiles Technology, Textiles and Design, Food Technology, Design and Technology, Technology Mandatory (Industrial Arts, Computing, Home Economics), alongside Engineering.

Out of all my teaching areas, Textiles is my heart and soul, and has been a main form of art and expression that I have utilised in my own secondary and tertiary education, alongside in my personal time. This includes projects like; art quilts, medieval dresses, felt banners, plush toys, backpacks, and textile art.
